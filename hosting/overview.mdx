Runpod is no longer accepting new hosts for Community Cloud. Existing hosts can continue to operate their hardware on the platform, and Community Cloud resources remain available to users.

If you are an existing host and need support, contact the Runpod team through your provider dashboard or at [support@runpod.io](mailto:support@runpod.io).

The following information applies to current Community Cloud hosts:

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Pricing** | GPU on-demand prices are consistent. Hosts can set minimum bid prices for spot rentals. |
| **Safety and trust** | All hosts must complete KYC verification. Larger providers require a Provider Agreement and SLA. |
| **Provider dashboard** | Manage resources, deploy hardware, and plan capacity through your dashboard. |
| **Rental rates** | Contact the Runpod team directly for GPU demand statistics and how hardware quality affects revenue. |
