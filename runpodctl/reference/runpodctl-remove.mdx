Permanently delete Runpod resources. Terminated Pods and their associated data cannot be recovered.

## Available commands

- [`runpodctl remove pod`](/runpodctl/reference/runpodctl-remove-pod) - Remove a single Pod by ID
- [`runpodctl remove pods`](/runpodctl/reference/runpodctl-remove-pods) - Remove multiple Pods by name
