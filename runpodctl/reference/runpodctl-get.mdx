Retrieve information about your Runpod resources, including Pods and available cloud GPUs.

## Available commands

- [`runpodctl get cloud`](/runpodctl/reference/runpodctl-get-cloud) - List all available cloud GPUs
- [`runpodctl get pod`](/runpodctl/reference/runpodctl-get-pod) - List all Pods or get details about a specific Pod
